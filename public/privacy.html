Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2, 2026.

Nightly Build is operated by Courtright Collective. We keep data collection to what an online store actually needs, and we do not sell your personal information. Ever.

What we collect

  • Order details: your name, shipping address, email, and phone, collected during checkout so we can print and ship your order.
  • Payment: handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your full card number. Stripe processes payment under its own privacy policy.
  • Cart data: stored locally in your browser so your cart survives a refresh. It never leaves your device until you check out.

Who we share it with

Only the partners required to fulfill your order: Stripe for payment and Printify and its print providers for production and shipping. They receive only what they need to do their part.

Cookies and analytics

We aim to run lean. Any analytics we use are privacy-respecting and aggregate. We do not run third-party ad trackers on this store.

Your choices

Want a copy of your data, or want it deleted? Email info@courtrightco.com and we will take care of it, subject to records we are legally required to keep.

Contact

Questions about privacy go to info@courtrightco.com.
